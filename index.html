Kimmie Van Pellecom

Pastry Enthusiast. Born in Brussels, July 1995. Raised in Antwerp. Moved to Stockholm, September 2016.

Have the most amazing parents and sister I could ever wish for. And the most supporting boyfriend I could ever dream of!
In Belgium I went to PIVA and did my course on Dessertkoekjes and in Stockholm I followed a course at Medborgarskolan called Konditor Utbildning.

Being part of the Millennials generation means having multiple social media platforms! ;) Give me a follow, a chat, a tweet and I'll be sure to follow, chat or tweet you back!

Pralines

I've learned how to make pralines during my class at Medborgarskolan.

Chocolate hearts

Ingredients
  1. Dark chocolate
  2. Cream
  3. Honey
  4. Butter
  5. Raspberry puree

These dark chocolate pralines where made at home, using techniques I learned from my teacher Nik at Medborgarskolan. The filling is based on a basic ganache filling which I adjusted to add the raspberry puree.

White chocolate hearts

Ingredients
  1. White chocolate
  2. Glucose
  3. Butter
  4. Raspberry puree
  5. Transfer sheet

These white chocolate pralines where made during my course at Medborgarskolan. Made using a transfer sheet for the decorations and a magnetic praline form. Inside is a raspberry ganache filling.

White chocolate cones

Ingredients
  1. White chocolate
  2. Glucose
  3. Butter
  4. Raspberry puree

These white chocolate pralines where made during my course at Medborgarskolan. Inside is a raspberry ganache filling.
